AGRI-VISION // v1.0

Agri-Vision is a satellite-powered early warning system that detects crop moisture stress before it becomes visible to the human eye. Using Sentinel-2 multispectral imagery from the European Space Agency's Copernicus programme, we compute the Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) to quantify vegetation health at the village level.

When our system detects a soil moisture anomaly exceeding the -15% threshold, it automatically dispatches alerts to the Block Development Officer (BDO), enabling proactive deployment of water tankers before crop failure occurs.

This dashboard monitors 6 target villages in Telangana, India, with data refreshed every 5 days per Sentinel-2 orbital pass.
